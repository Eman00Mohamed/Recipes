Cold chocolate truffle

Ingredients

  • Chocolate: 250 grams (chopped)
  • Cream: half a cup (liquid)
  • Butter: 2 tablespoons
  • Cocoa powder: half a cup (for decoration)

recipe

Heat the cream and butter in a non-stick saucepan over medium heat until it starts to boil.
Add the chocolate, and stir until melted and the mixture is smooth.
Remove the saucepan from the heat, stir with a plastic spoon, and leave the mixture until it freezes, then put it in a bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and put it in the refrigerator for about 3 hours.
Remove the bowl from the refrigerator, and form small balls from the mixture.
Dip the balls in cocoa, place them on a serving plate, and serve.

Paris Brest

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk.
  • 1 cup flour.
  • 3 eggs.
  • 3 tablespoons butter.
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.
  • 1 tablespoon sugar.
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger.
  • 5 cherries.
  • 2 cups whipping cream

recipe

First, put the milk on the fire until it boils.
Then add the butter and leave it until it melts completely.
Add sugar, salt, ginger and cinnamon to the previous mixture and stir these ingredients well.
Then remove the mixture from the fire and leave it aside until it cools.
The dough is placed in a greased tray in the shape of a rectangle.
The tray is placed in a hot oven and left until it puffs up.
Finally, the tray is taken out of the oven and the Paris Brest is filled with Chantilly cream and decorated with cherries.

Cassoulet

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes cut into cubes, can be replaced with tomato sauce.
  • 1 kilo of fresh pork sausage, can be replaced with beef or lamb.
  • 4 cups of homemade chicken broth.
  • A bunch of parsley, thyme, black peppercorns, spices and bay leaves.
  • Half a cup of breadcrumbs.
  • Half a cup of beans.
  • 2 duck confit legs.
  • A quarter cup of beans.

recipe

First, soak the beans in a large bowl. Add the beans, salt, and two cups of water to the previous mixture and leave them at room temperature.

Then cut the meat into small pieces and add 6 cups of water and leave on the fire until it boils for about half an hour.

After making sure it is cooked, break up the outer shell using a wooden spoon and repeat this process several times with the addition of chicken broth if the recipe dries up, and serve it hot.

Croissant

Ingredients

  • Yeast 25 grams (g)
  • Milk 1 cup
  • Egg 1 piece
  • Salt 0.5 teaspoon
  • Ice sugar 40 grams (g)
  • Flour 380 grams (g)
  • Egg 1 piece
  • Water 2 teaspoons

recipe

Cut the butter into about eight slices.
In a bowl, mash the yeast and mix with the milk.
Add the eggs, salt, sugar and flour.
Quickly form the dough.
Let it rest in the fridge for about 20 minutes.
Put the dough out on a floured baking sheet about 30 x 40 cm in size.
Spread the butter on half of the dough, 2-3 cm from the edges.
Fold the edges of the dough over the butter first, then fold the dough in half.
Press the edges together.
Carefully roll out the dough into a rectangle about 1 cm thick.
Fold the dough three times (i.e. two folds towards the middle to form three layers of dough).
Cover with plastic wrap and leave in the fridge for about 30 minutes.
Repeat the process twice.
Put the dough out into a rectangle about 20 cm long and 5 mm thick.
Cut into triangles with a base of 10 cm.
Shape the croissants by rolling the dough from the widest side of the triangle.
Place each croissant on a baking sheet lined with baking paper, edge down.
Prepare the egg wash by whisking together the egg and water.
Gently brush each croissant with the egg wash.
Cover with plastic wrap and leave to rise at room temperature for 1 to 1 1/2 hours (depending on the room temperature).
Preheat the oven to 225°C.
When the croissants have finished rising, gently brush each pastry again with the egg wash.
Bake in the middle of the oven until golden, about 12 minutes.

Strawberry Cinnamon French Toast

Ingredients

  • Milk 1 cup
  • Puck Whipping Cream
  • Whipping Cream
  • 0.5 cup Egg
  • 3 vanilla pods
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons strawberries, halved
  • 1 cup Puck Squeeze Cheese
  • Squeeze Cheese 0.5 cup
  • Honey 0.5 cup

recipe

Mix together milk, Puck whipping cream, sugar, vanilla and cinnamon until well combined.
Soak the bread slices in this mixture for 5 to 7 minutes.
In a non-stick pan, melt a little butter, then cook the soaked bread slices for 1 to 2 minutes on each side until golden.
Repeat this step with the remaining bread slices.
Place the toasted French bread on serving plates, top with Puck Squeeze Cheese, garnish with strawberries, then drizzle with honey and enjoy.

Chicken with lemon ginger cream sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast fillet, medium boneless and skinless 500 grams (g)
  • Water 0.25 cups
  • Butter 2 tablespoons
  • Olive oil 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic cloves, minced 2 pieces
  • Onion, diced 0.3 cups
  • Fresh ginger, grated 1 tablespoon
  • Lemon juice 0.25 cups
  • Lemon peel 1 teaspoon
  • Chicken stock 1 cup
  • Cooking cream
  • 0.5 cups Hot chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Green onions, thinly sliced

recipe

Cut each chicken breast in half and gently pound between two sheets of plastic wrap, using a rolling pin, to ensure even thickness for even cooking.
Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
Heat a heavy-bottomed, nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
Add 1 tablespoon butter and 1 tablespoon olive oil to the skillet.
Sear the chicken strips on one side without moving until golden.
After 2 minutes, flip the chicken strips over and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
Reduced heat to medium, added ¼ cup water, and covered for an additional 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
Remove chicken from skillet, cover, and set aside.
Using the same skillet, add the remaining butter and olive oil.
Sauté the onion over medium heat for 1 minute.
Add the garlic, ginger, and chili flakes, and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
Add the chicken stock, lemon zest, and juice to the skillet.
Bring to a boil, then stir in Puck Cooking Cream.
Add salt and pepper to the sauce.
Return the chicken breasts to the pan and cook with the sauce for 5 minutes.
Turn off the heat, garnish with green onions, and serve with a green salad or rice.

Crunchy French Toast Churro Fingers

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of brioche bread
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • Corn flakes
  • Butter
  • 1 ½ cups cinnamon sugar (300 g)

recipe

Remove the crusts from the bread.
Cut the bread into large squares.
Crush the cornflakes in a bag, pour into a shallow dish and set aside.
Mix the eggs, milk, brown sugar, cinnamon and vanilla in a bowl.
Heat a skillet with about 1 tablespoon of butter.
Dip the bread into the egg mixture on all sides.
Then dip into the crushed cornflakes.
Sear the churros in the skillet until golden brown on all sides.
Immediately roll in the cinnamon sugar mixture.
Serve with syrup or chocolate sauce for dipping
